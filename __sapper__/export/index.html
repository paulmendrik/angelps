About

Jacqui Angel BA (hons), MCIPD has more than 25 years experience in HR and Recruitment, having worked across the Public and Private sectors, dealing with types of roles from the most junior entry level roles to the most senior Chief Executive or Director level posts.

Her focus is very much on quality matching and retention of staff rather than ‘quick fix’ options and she combines her knowledge of best practice with a commercial, pragmatic approach. Jacqui works tirelessly to find the best solutions for clients. She seeks to help organisations through periods of peak recruitment or where making an informed, robust selection decision is critical. She also seeks to help individuals who are looking for a change in career direction or are looking to enter/ reenter the job market and are not sure which way to turn.

Services

Recruitment and Selection including application screening, assessment centre design and delivery, psychometric testing/ profiling, interviewing, career transition work.

Jacqui Angel is trained in the use of ability testing and personality profiling including OPQ, Wave, LJI and the Myers Briggs Type Indicator and registered to purchase tests or set candidates up for online testing with all the major test publishers.
